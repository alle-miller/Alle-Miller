About Me

I am a graphic designer based in Columbia, Missouri, focusing on marketing and visual communication. My work centers on creating clear, strategic designs for digital and print platforms, with an emphasis on audience engagement, messaging, and maintaining visual consistency across brand materials.

I am pursuing a BA in Graphic Design with a minor in Marketing at Columbia College. Through graphic design internships at Central Bank and Visionworks Marketing Group, I have developed experience in branding, marketing materials, social media graphics, and visual campaigns.

I'm passionate about creating designs that not only look beautiful but also effectively communicate the intended message and drive results for businesses and organizations.
